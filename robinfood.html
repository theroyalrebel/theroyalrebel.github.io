Robin Food

An app to reduce food waste by connecting supermarkets with excess food to food banks

The concept

Observations

By conducting field trips to food bank headquarters and supermarkets, we had the opportunity to observe the food handling processes and interview key stakeholders

We noted main areas that our app would be useful in

  • Coordinating food surplus collections between multiple parties and reducing time spent on phone calls to arrange pickups
  • Reporting live statistics and summaries of food surplus data to food donors with multiple branches
  • Helping food banks digitally store paperwork (eg:supermarket merchandise exit forms) for fast retrieval

Food surplus posting process

As the main users of the app are supermarket managers whose main priorities are handling supermarket operations, we streamlined the posting process to have as few steps and options as necessary


List of transactions

Pickup info page

As there were 4 main users of the app - the donors, donor HQ if part of a bigger chain, food bank and food bank drivers, we came up with a single page for each transaction that would update live as more information was made available
