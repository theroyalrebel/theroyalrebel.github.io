About Me

​Hi there! I'm Crystal, a student from Malaysia pursuing my studies in Philadelphia.

​I've been a graphic designer for 7 years and was first introduced to the field of user interface and user experience design in 2014 when I had to prepare app mockups for the startup I co-founded, MyEdvisor. I was intrigued by the myriad of decisions that one could make while organizing information on a screen for users and I enjoyed the process of envisioning the big picture of the user workflow while working on small interaction details. In my most recent internship with the Sime Darby group, I valued the opportunities to interview potential users of our app Robin Food, develop prototypes to be used by them, and implement their feedback in next iterations. My ambitions are simply to improve existing processes, or design optimized solutions for current problems.

​My greatest passion is photography, which goes with my love for traveling. I seek to capture the beauty of the places that I visit. I spend countless hours researching the best vantage points, planning the most efficient travel routes to hit those places, and camping out at spots waiting for the perfect moment.

​I also love running and hiking and I'm training for my first half marathon this November
